SETTLEMENT OF PAYMENT

FOR LEGAL ADVICE
It is a one-time fee for legal advice and is normally PLN 170 gross (already including VAT). In the case of advice on more complex matters that require more time to analyze the legal status, the price may differ, which will be communicated before legal advice is provided.
FOR ACTION/CASE
Means remuneration for performing a specific activity (e.g. preparation of a lawsuit, legal opinion, draft contract, appeal) or a case (court or administrative, court-administrative proceedings) in the amount agreed individually with the client.
HOURLY PAY
It is the product of the working time devoted to the activity or case and the hourly rate agreed individually (previously approved) with the client. The above method of settlement is addressed primarily to business entities or individuals who occasionally use the services of the Law Firm. When determining the amount of the hourly rate, the complexity and type of the case are taken into account.
FLAT FEE
A fixed monthly remuneration for the number of hours set out in the contract with the client for the legal services provided by the Law Firm (e.g. a fixed amount of PLN 3,000 + VAT). It is intended for entrepreneurs who have permanent needs in the field of comprehensive legal services for their business activity.
